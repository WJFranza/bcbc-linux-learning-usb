Quick Distro Guide

This page helps users choose a Linux environment based on comfort level, hardware, and goals.

Start Page Launch GUI AI Help

Ubuntu

Best all-around starting point. Easy to find help, good hardware support, and a smooth first experience.

Zorin OS Core

Good for people used to Windows who want something familiar and comfortable.

Xubuntu

Best choice when the computer is older or resources are limited.

Debian

Great for users who want a cleaner, more technician-oriented environment and less hand-holding.

Kubuntu

Strong pick for users who like a more customizable desktop and a polished interface.

Garuda

Excellent for users who want a bold look, modern styling, and a more adventurous feel.
